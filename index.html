Fabric Calculator

Privacy Policy — Last updated March 16, 2025

Fabric Calculator ("the App") is developed by Vincent Washington. This privacy policy explains how the App handles your information.

Information We Collect

Fabric Calculator does not collect, transmit, or store any personal information on external servers. All data you enter — including garment measurements, style numbers, fabric yields, and history — is stored locally on your device only.

AI Estimates

When you use the AI estimation feature, garment measurement data is sent to Anthropic's API to generate a fabric yield estimate. No personally identifiable information is included in these requests. Please refer to Anthropic's Privacy Policy for details on how they handle API data.

Data Storage

All app data including your style library, history, and confirmed yields is stored locally on your device using AsyncStorage. This data never leaves your device except as described above in AI Estimates.

Third Party Services

The App uses Anthropic's Claude API for AI-powered fabric yield estimates. No other third party services are used.

Children's Privacy

The App is not directed at children under 13 and we do not knowingly collect information from children.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated date.
